Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Register" link in the navigation menu and filling out the registration form with your details.

How do I add books to my collection?

Once logged in, navigate to the "Add Book" page from your dashboard. Fill in the book details and submit the form to add it to your collection.

Can I share my book collection with others?

Yes! You can share your collection or specific reading lists by using the share feature on your profile page.

Is BibTech free to use?

BibTech offers both free and premium plans. The basic features are free for all users, while advanced features require a premium subscription.
